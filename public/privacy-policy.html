Privacy Policy – SSC Exam Sathi

Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how SSC Exam Sathi (“we”, “the app”) handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information stored on your device

The app stores the following data only on your device (local storage):

  • Your syllabus progress (which topics or subtopics you have marked as completed)
  • Your selected exam (e.g. SSC CGL)
  • Your language preference (English or Hindi)
  • In-app achievement milestones (e.g. 25%, 50% completion)
  • Theme preference (light or dark mode)

This data is not automatically sent to any server. Backup and restore is done by you (e.g. exporting a file to your storage); we do not upload or store your progress on our servers.

2. Analytics (Firebase)

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used. This may collect:

  • Anonymous usage events (e.g. screen views, which exam was selected, topic completion toggles, milestones reached)
  • Device type and general usage patterns

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personally identifiable information. Analytics data is used only to improve the app and is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy and Firebase terms.

3. No account or sign-in

The app does not require you to create an account or sign in. All progress is stored locally on your device.

4. No sale of data

We do not sell your personal data or your progress data to third parties.

5. Data you choose to share

If you use the “Back up progress” feature, you choose where to save the backup file (e.g. your device storage or cloud). We do not have access to those files. If you use “Restore from backup”, the app only reads the file you select on your device.

6. Uninstalling the app

If you uninstall the app, all data stored on your device (including progress) is removed. To keep your progress, use “Back up progress” before uninstalling and “Restore from backup” after reinstalling.

7. Children

The app is intended for general use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will address it.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the app’s data practices, you can contact us via:

  • Telegram: t.me/warriorsiq
  • Or through the links provided in the app (e.g. YouTube, Instagram).
